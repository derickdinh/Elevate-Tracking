Frequently Asked Questions

When is Elevate launching?

We’re putting the finishing touches on Elevate and plan to launch in Q4 2025. Exact dates are still being finalized due to ongoing testing and feature enhancements. Join our waitlist to get the latest updates as soon as we lock in the launch day!

How can I suggest a feature?

We’d love to hear your ideas! Use the form at the bottom of this page to submit your suggestions, or email us directly at hello@elevatetracking.com. Our team reviews every submission, and some of our best features have come from community input—so don’t hold back!

What devices will Elevate support?

Elevate is being developed for iOS, with compatibility planned for Android in the future. We’re also exploring web app support post-launch. Follow our social media for updates on device compatibility as we near release!
