4-Day Upper/Lower Hypertrophy Workout Split

Maximize muscle growth with targeted exercises, sets, and rest periods

Workout Schedule

Day Workout
Monday Upper A
Tuesday Lower
Thursday Upper B
Friday Lower

Upper A (Monday)

1. Cable Lat Pulldown (Bar Wide Grip)

Target Muscle: Lats

Rest: 180s between sets

Sets:

  • Warmup: 8 reps (50% working weight)
  • Warmup: 4 reps (70% working weight)
  • Working: 5-8 reps

Notes: Pull by driving your elbows to the side of your body and keep your body still and slightly leaned back.

2. Seated Row Machine (Overhand Wide Grip) - Selector

Target Muscle: Upper Back

Rest: 180s between sets

Sets:

  • Warmup: 4 reps (70% working weight)
  • Working: 5-8 reps

Notes: Pull with your elbows flared, at a little below 90°. Make sure you retract your scapula, squeezing your shoulder blades at the back and let your shoulder blades pull apart at the start.

3. Smith Machine Incline Bench Press

Target Muscle: Chest

Rest: 180s between sets

Sets:

  • Warmup: 8 reps (50% working weight)
  • Warmup: 4 reps (70% working weight)
  • Working: 5-8 reps

Notes: Hold the bar a little outside shoulder width with the bar hitting around low to mid chest at the bottom. Make sure your elbows are semi-tucked at the bottom at around 45°.

4. Chest Press Machine - Selector

Target Muscle: Chest

Rest: 180s between sets

Sets:

  • Warmup: 4 reps (70% working weight)
  • Working: 5-8 reps

5. Cable Lateral Raise

Target Muscle: Shoulders

Rest: 180s between sets

Sets:

  • Warmup (Left): 8 reps (50% working weight)
  • Warmup (Right): 8 reps (50% working weight)
  • Warmup (Left): 4 reps (70% working weight)
  • Warmup (Right): 4 reps (70% working weight)
  • Working (Left): 5-8 reps
  • Working (Right): 5-8 reps

Notes: Perform the weaker side first. Set cable to around hand height and raise to the sides and slightly in front of your body.

6. Seated Shoulder Press Machine (Selector)

Target Muscle: Shoulders

Rest: 180s between sets

Sets:

  • Warmup: 4 reps (70% working weight)
  • Working: 5-8 reps

7. Preacher Curl Machine - Selector

Target Muscle: Biceps

Rest: 180s between sets

Sets:

  • Warmup: 4 reps (70% working weight)
  • Working: 5-8 reps

Notes: Keep your chest and upper arms pressed against the pad.

8. Preacher Curl Machine - Selector (Single Arm)

Target Muscle: Biceps

Rest: 180s between sets

Sets:

  • Working (Left): 5-8 reps
  • Working (Right): 5-8 reps

Notes: Perform weaker side first.

9. Cable Tricep Pushdown (Rope)

Target Muscle: Triceps

Rest: 180s between sets

Sets:

  • Warmup: 4 reps (70% working weight)
  • Working: 5-8 reps

Notes: Keep your elbows to the side of your body and push the rope down without moving your elbows.

10. Cable Tricep Pushdown (Rope) - Single Arm

Target Muscle: Triceps

Rest: 180s between sets

Sets:

  • Working (Left): 5-8 reps
  • Working (Right): 5-8 reps

Notes: Perform weaker side first. Use a cuff or a fabric D-handle with a neutral grip and hold cable tower for stability.

Upper B (Thursday)

1. Cable Tricep Pushdown (Bar)

Target Muscle: Triceps

Rest: 180s between sets

Sets:

  • Warmup: 8 reps (50% working weight)
  • Warmup: 4 reps (70% working weight)
  • Working: 5-8 reps
  • Working: 5-8 reps

Notes: As you push down, let your elbows push forward in front of your body a bit instead of keeping your elbows fixed at the side of your body.

2. Face Away Cable Curl (Bar)

Target Muscle: Biceps

Rest: 180s between sets

Sets:

  • Warmup: 8 reps (50% working weight)
  • Warmup: 4 reps (70% working weight)
  • Working: 5-8 reps
  • Working: 5-8 reps

Notes: Keep your elbows beside your body and stand straight.

3. Dumbbell Lateral Raise

Target Muscle: Shoulders

Rest: 180s between sets

Sets:

  • Warmup: 8 reps (50% working weight)
  • Warmup: 4 reps (70% working weight)
  • Working: 5-8 reps
  • Working: 5-8 reps

Notes: Lean slightly forward and raise to your side and slightly forward.

4. Smith Machine Incline Bench Press

Target Muscle: Chest

Rest: 180s between sets

Sets:

  • Warmup: 8 reps (50% working weight)
  • Warmup: 4 reps (70% working weight)
  • Working: 5-8 reps

Notes: Hold the bar a little outside shoulder width with the bar hitting around low to mid chest at the bottom. Make sure your elbows are semi-tucked at the bottom at around 45°.

5. Pec Deck Machine

Target Muscle: Chest

Rest: 180s between sets

Sets:

  • Warmup (Left): 4 reps (70% working weight)
  • Warmup (Right): 4 reps (70% working weight)
  • Working (Left): 5-8 reps
  • Working (Right): 5-8 reps

Notes: Perform weaker side first. Start with elbows slightly bent and straighten your elbows as you bring your hands together. Set seat height so that the handles line up with mid to low chest.

6. Seated Row Machine (Neutral Grip) - Selector

Target Muscle: Lats

Rest: 180s between sets

Sets:

  • Warmup (Left): 8 reps (50% working weight)
  • Warmup (Right): 8 reps (50% working weight)
  • Warmup (Left): 4 reps (70% working weight)
  • Warmup (Right): 4 reps (70% working weight)
  • Working (Left): 5-8 reps
  • Working (Right): 5-8 reps

Notes: Perform weaker side first. Pull with your elbows tucked in to your body.

7. Seated Row Machine (Overhand Wide Grip) - Selector

Target Muscle: Upper Back

Rest: 180s between sets

Sets:

  • Warmup: 4 reps (70% working weight)
  • Working: 5-8 reps

Notes: Pull with your elbows flared, at a little below 90°. Make sure you retract your scapula, squeezing your shoulder blades at the back and let your shoulder blades pull apart at the start.

Important Program Notes

  • Weight Selection: Choose a weight that challenges you but allows you to maintain proper form. Aim for progressive overload over time.
  • Rest Times: Stick to the 180-second (3-minute) rest periods between sets to maximize recovery and performance. You can skip rest between warm-ups for efficiency.
  • Form: Follow the exercise notes carefully to ensure proper technique and reduce injury risk.
